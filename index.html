About Me

Hi, I'm Amira.

I'm a product-minded technologist with an engineering background and expertise in cybersecurity, AI, and human-centered design—building products that solve real problems for real people.

Over the past several years, I've worked across data analytics at Thomson Reuters and full-stack engineering and cybersecurity operations at Target, building systems that protect millions of users daily. I've led initiatives that reduced critical response times from days to minutes, shipped features adopted by 100% of users in four weeks, and built tools that engineering teams integrated without mandates or training.

Before Target, I built data discovery platforms for 500+ users at Thomson Reuters, won grant funding for a healthcare equity platform (ENIAC Pioneer Award), and learned that the best products are the ones users don't notice—they just work.

I earned Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science with a concentration in Computing and I'm currently pursuing my Microsoft AI Product Manager Certification.
