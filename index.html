About Me

Hi, I'm Amira.

I’m a product minded technologist with an engineering background and expertise in cybersecurity, AI, and human centered design; building products that solve real problems for real people.

Over the past several years, I have worked across data analytics at Thomson Reuters and full stack engineering and cybersecurity operations at Target, partnering with engineering, security, and analytics teams to ship impactful products. I have led initiatives that significantly accelerated critical response times, delivered features rapidly adopted across the entire user base, and built internal platforms that scaled across multiple engineering teams to improve deployment reliability and operational efficiency.

In parallel with my product and security work, I've developed deep expertise in AI and machine learning, building generative AI agents, presenting on AI-assisted threat analysis and prompt engineering best practices at internal cybersecurity conferences, and creating predictive models including binary classification systems for credit approval. My work spans practical implementation (LLMs, RAG, embeddings) and theoretical understanding (computer vision, deep learning algorithms), positioning me to lead AI product development that balances innovation with responsibility.

Previously, I built data discovery platforms for business users at Thomson Reuters, built a healthcare equity platform recognized with the ENIAC Pioneer Award, and developed a strong belief that the most effective security systems are the ones users barely notice because they work reliably in the background.

I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science with a concentration in Computing and I recently completed Enterprise Product Management Fundamentals - Microsoft AI Product Manager Professional Certificate, continuing to merge product strategy, AI innovation, and cybersecurity excellence.
